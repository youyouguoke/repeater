Audio Repeater Online was built by a team of musicians, language learners, and developers who were frustrated with the lack of simple, privacy-focused audio looping tools.

We believe that learning through repetition is one of the most effective techniques for mastering music, languages, and any auditory skill. Our mission is to make professional-grade audio looping accessible to everyone, completely free and without compromising your privacy.

Unlike other tools that require software installation or upload your files to remote servers, Audio Repeater Online processes everything locally in your browser. Your audio never leaves your computer.
