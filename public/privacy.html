Your privacy is our top priority.

All audio processing happens locally in your browser using Web Audio API. We do not upload, store, or process your audio files on any server. Your files never leave your computer.

The only data we collect is anonymous usage analytics through Google Analytics to help us improve the tool. This data cannot be used to identify you.

By using Audio Repeater Online, you can be confident that your audio content remains completely private and secure.
