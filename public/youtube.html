What Is YouTube Looper?

A YouTube looper is an essential tool for anyone who wants to repeat part of a YouTube video without manually clicking replay. Whether you are trying to loop a guitar solo, repeat a difficult language phrase, or review a coding tutorial step by step, our online YouTube video repeater makes it effortless. Simply paste your link, set the A/B loop points, and let the video repeat automatically. You can also slow down YouTube video playback to 0.25x or speed it up to 2x, giving you complete control over your learning pace. Unlike browser extensions, our YouTube practice tool works on any device with a browser — no installation, no sign-up, and completely free. Students use it to repeat lectures, musicians use it to isolate solos, dancers use it to break down choreography, and developers use it to follow along with coding tutorials.
